Quickly and easily extract text from your PDF files in your browser. No accounts necessary.

How to Convert PDF to Text

Simply drag and drop your PDF file into the area above or click to select a file from your device. The extracted text will be displayed below.

FAQs

Is this service free?

Yes, this tool is completely free to use.

Do you store my files?

No, this tool works entirely in your browser without any cloud storage or servers.

My PDF looks garbled. Why?

This tool uses the "metadata" of the pdf to find the text on the page. It does not do "optical character recognition". Your PDF may not have the necessary metadata for this tool to extract the text.
